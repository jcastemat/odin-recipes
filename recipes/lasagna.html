Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort dish that layers rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and perfectly cooked pasta sheets into a hearty, flavorful bake. Each bite delivers a perfect balance of savory ground beef, aromatic herbs, tangy tomato sauce, and gooey melted cheese. It’s a timeless favorite that brings warmth to any table.

Whether you're making it for a family dinner, a cozy weekend meal, or just to meal prep for the week, lasagna is both satisfying and versatile. You can stick to the traditional meat version or switch it up with vegetables, spinach, or even plant-based alternatives. Either way, this dish is sure to be a crowd-pleaser! 🍝✨

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2–3 garlic cloves
  • 800g canned crushed tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper
  • 300g shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles: Boil lasagna noodles in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the sauce: In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté the onion and garlic. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Let simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  3. Assemble the layers: In a baking dish, start with a thin layer of sauce. Then layer: noodles → meat sauce → mozzarella. Repeat until ingredients are used, ending with sauce and mozzarella on top.
  4. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for 10–15 more minutes until bubbly and golden. Let it rest for about 15 minutes and enjoy!
