Ingredients

  • 4 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1/2 green bell pepper
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Blend it all: In a blender, combine tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, garlic, olive oil, vinegar, and a pinch of salt. Blend until very smooth.
  2. Chill: Refrigerate for at least 1 hour. Gazpacho is best served cold.
  3. Serve: Pour into bowls or glasses, drizzle with olive oil, and enjoy! Optionally top with diced veggies or croutons.
